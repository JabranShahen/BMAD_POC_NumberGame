# Story: Apply Sunset Punch Palette

Epic: Epic 1 - Core Gameplay Loop (Visual polish)  
Goal: Adopt a bold, lively palette so buttons and badges stand out without hurting readability.

Palette (Sunset Punch):
- Primary (CTAs/buttons): Harley Davidson Orange
- Secondary/ground: Seabrook or Mazarine Blue for contrast
- Accent (badges/links): Nanohanacha Gold (used sparingly)
- Surface/neutral: Hint of Pensive or Lynx White
- Text: Electromagnetic (dark) on light; white on dark

Acceptance Criteria:
1) Buttons: Primary filled = Harley Davidson Orange with white text; hover/press darkens; focus ring visible. Secondary outlined with Seabrook/Mazarine stroke; hover fills lightly; focus ring visible.
2) Badges: Best/Last tags use gold or muted orange; text high-contrast.
3) Header/App bars: Gradient or solid using Seabrook/Mazarine; text/icons white.
4) Surfaces: Cards on light neutral; avoid monochrome buttons; apply palette to difficulty screen CTAs.
5) Accessibility: All combinations meet WCAG AA (check primary/badge text contrast).
6) Consistency: Apply palette across difficulty cards, CTAs, badges, header.
